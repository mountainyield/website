MOUNTAIN SIDE GROWERS — IMAGE DOWNLOAD LIST
============================================

HOW THIS WORKS
--------------
Right now every image on the site loads from your live Squarespace address,
so the photos show up immediately (in preview and after you deploy). Each
<img> first looks for a LOCAL copy in an "images/" folder; if it doesn't
find one, it falls back to the Squarespace URL automatically.

That fallback ONLY works while your Squarespace site is still online. So
BEFORE you cancel the Squarespace WEBSITE plan, download the 10 images below,
save them with the exact filenames shown, and put them in a folder named
"images" next to your .html files. Once they're there, the site uses your
local copies and no longer depends on Squarespace at all.
