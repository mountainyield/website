Sustainably Grown, Rooted in Nature

Living Soil Cultivation

We nurture our cannabis in living soil rich with beneficial microbes and organic matter, mimicking the natural ecosystem. This method promotes plant vitality, enhances terpene profiles, and yields a more flavorful, potent flower — just as nature intended.

Eco-Conscious Water & Air Practices

Our cultivation process uses precision watering systems and efficient air circulation to minimize waste and maintain ideal growing conditions. This ensures healthy plants while reducing our environmental footprint every step of the way.

Natural Inputs Only

We never cut corners — our plants are nourished with all-natural fertilizers and protected with organic pest management solutions. No synthetics, no harsh chemicals — just clean, conscientious cultivation from seed to harvest.

About Our Company

About our company

Mountain Side Growers, LLC is a family-owned and operated cannabis micro grower based in the heart of New Mexico. As proud New Mexico natives, we're deeply connected to the land and committed to cultivating cannabis that reflects the integrity and richness of our region. With a dedication to craftsmanship and care, we focus on producing small-batch, premium flower that meets the highest standards for quality, consistency, and purity.

At the core of our cultivation philosophy is a deep respect for the environment. We grow all our plants using living soil practices that support vibrant microbial life and a thriving root ecosystem. Every step of our process is designed with sustainability in mind — from natural pest management and organic fertilizers to eco-conscious watering and airflow systems. At Mountain Side Growers, we believe that the best cannabis is grown in harmony with nature — and it shows in every bud we produce.
